Glimpse Social Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 7, 2020

The Glimpse Social Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand the information we collect and the different ways we may use or share it. This policy applies to your use of Glimpse Social. By “Glimpse Social,” we mean the Glimpse Social website, browser extension, mobile app, and any other of our products and services that directly link to this policy.

The Information We Collect

You probably wonder what information we collect. This often depends on the exact nature of our relationship with you and how you choose to use Glimpse Social, but we’ve described the most common examples below. We have broken these descriptions down into categories to make them clearer:
We collect account information. We collect information relating to your account. For example, we collect basic registration details like name, email address, and username and password when you create a Glimpse Social account. You may be able to share other account-related information with us too, like if you choose to provide a profile photo or telephone number. We collect some information about your friends when you invite or refer them to Glimpse Social or import your contacts.
We collect usage information. We collect information about your interactions with and on Glimpse Social. Glimpse Social uses this info to make the product better for you so you. We want to do the heavy lifting, utilizing tech to improve the experience.
We collect certain information when you link third party accounts or apps to Glimpse Social. If you link social media accounts to Glimpse Social, we collect certain social media account information. If you link your contacts we collect information about your friends, like their phone numbers and addresses.
We collect location information. For example, based on your IP address, we derive location information.
We collect feedback information. We collect information that you give us when you complete a survey or participate in a contest or promotion. This includes when you write a review or make a suggestion. Feedback can be submitted at support@glimpsesocial.com
We collect device information. We collect information about the browser or device you are using. We may look at what site you came from, or what site you visit when you leave us. We may also collect device identifiers. For example, this might include your operating system and hardware model. This may also include your mobile carrier.

We use some information to communicate with you. We use information to respond to your questions, including questions about our services or your relationship with us, and to communicate with you about this policy or our Terms. We may also contact you about your feedback.
We just want to be able to be responsive to you, and occasionally reach out to hear how it’s going on Glimpse Social, and make sure you have updates on what’s going on with us!

With your permission where required by law, for example, we use information to suggest other users with whom you may want to connect. We use information to make Glimpse Social better, for example by saving certain preferences you choose, tracking events in Glimpse Social, and recording other performance data.
